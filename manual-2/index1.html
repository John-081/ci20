PS4 MANUAL

1. TURN ON YOUR PS4

Find the power switch on your PS4. Press on it.

2. CONNECT YOUR CONTROLLER

Find the PS4 logo on your controller. Press on it.

3. CHOOSE YOUR GAME

Use the directional buttons on your controller to select your game on PS4 menu.

PLAY TIME!

Use the x buttons on your controller to comfirm the game you selected.
